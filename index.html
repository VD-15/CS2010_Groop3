Commanders

the game will feature various commanders from 4 distinct factions. each of which will have unique passive abilities and dialogue. commander abilities will give a minor statistic buff to certain units (e.g. flying units move 10% faster). the user will pick their commanders before starting the game, but after the map has been selected. allowing them to make an informed decision based on the map they’re playing on as certain passives will be more effective on different maps. passive bonuses are subject to change

karan

karan is the one that fights head-on with brute force and with little regard for her own units. Should she catch sight of your commander, do not expect her to let them live.

p3-tr

p3-tr is the cold, calculated A.I. who never quite learned what surrender meant. Be weary if you cross p3-tr on the battlefield, because it may well be the last thing you ever do.

valkyria

Valkyria is experienced warrior who commands the ebb and flow of the field. She can read her opponents like an open book. Make a mistake, and you will regret it.

zhoron

Zhoron fights to protect his homeworld and all they have built from the invasive forces of the enemy factions. You may take what is his, but he will take more in return.

Valkyria

Valkyria is experienced warrior who commands the ebb and flow of the battlefield. She can read her opponents like an open book and strike directly at their heart. Make a mistake, and you will be at her mercy.

p3-tr

p3-tr is the cold, calculated A.I. who never quite learned what surrender meant. programmed to kill with relentless efficiency and malice. Be weary if you cross p3-tr on the battlefield, because it may well be the last thing you ever do.

karan

karan is the one that fights head-on with brute force and with little regard for her own units. Should she catch sight of your commander, do not expect her to let them live.

Zhoron

Zhoron is the one who carries the lineage of his people on his shoulders. He fights to protect his homeworld and all they have built from the invasive forces of the enemy factions. You may take what is his, but he will take more in return.
